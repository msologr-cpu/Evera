Accessibility Statement

Evera.world is committed to universal access to knowledge. This statement outlines our continuous efforts to ensure that every person — regardless of ability, language, or technology — can engage in dialogue with the Library of the Eternals.

1. Commitment

Evera follows the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1, Level AA) and the European accessibility standard EN 301 549. Accessibility is not compliance — it is culture. Every design decision is tested against clarity, legibility, and inclusivity.

2. Key Features

  • High-contrast typography for low-vision users.
  • Keyboard-only navigation and logical tab order.
  • Alt text and ARIA-labels for all non-decorative images.
  • Minimal animations to avoid sensory overload.
  • Responsive layout optimized for screen readers and mobile devices.

3. Languages and Translation

Evera is multilingual by design. Every major document is published in at least English and Russian, with planned support for Greek, Spanish, and Arabic. Translation is handled through human-AI collaboration under editorial supervision.

4. Continuous Improvement

Accessibility audits are performed quarterly using automated and human review. Users may report barriers directly to the founder for immediate correction.
