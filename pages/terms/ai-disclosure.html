AI Transparency & Identity Disclaimer

This document clarifies how Evera.world creates, manages, and presents digital dialogues with historical minds — the “Eternals.” Evera is not a simulation of living persons; it is a scientific and philosophical framework for dialogical reconstruction of knowledge.

1. Nature of AI Reconstructions

Each “Eternal” is built upon verified linguistic, historical, and psychological sources. Dialogues are generated using the Evera Dialogical Reconstruction System — a framework combining symbolic modeling, contextual AI, and curated historical datasets. The system produces reconstructions of intellectual methods, not personalities.

2. Identity Disclaimer

No digital model represents or imitates any living individual. Names of historical figures are used strictly for educational and cultural purposes. All dialogues explicitly indicate their AI-generated nature. Users are reminded that these digital voices speak from historical data, not from the consciousness of the original individuals.

3. Transparency Principles

  • Every AI model includes a public disclosure of its data sources and historical references.
  • All dialogues are labeled with the identity of the AI agent (“module of the Evera Library”).
  • Evera prohibits any modification or repurposing of its models for commercial or manipulative use.

4. Ethical Oversight

Evera operates under the Ethics Charter, which enforces human dignity, truthfulness, and scientific transparency. Mentors and curators review every new digital reconstruction before public release.

5. Data Handling

Dialogue data is anonymized. No user interactions are used for behavioral profiling or external AI training. Aggregated anonymous metrics are used solely for research on educational impact.

6. Intellectual Property

The Evera Dialogical Reconstruction System (“Evera Format”) is an original intellectual creation protected under international copyright and database rights (Directive 96/9/EC, Berne Convention). No derivative works or AI training on Evera data are permitted without written consent of the author.

7. Cultural Sensitivity

Evera respects cultural, religious, and philosophical diversity. The project refrains from political interpretations of the past. AI reconstructions aim to preserve wisdom, not ideology.
