Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Evera.world uses cookies and similar technologies to ensure optimal performance, accessibility, and ethical transparency.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help a website function properly. Evera uses only essential cookies that maintain your chosen language, accessibility settings, and session state.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Functional Cookies — required for the operation of Evera and your ability to navigate pages.
  • Preference Cookies — store your display and language preferences.
  • Security Cookies — used temporarily to protect against automated attacks and ensure server integrity.

3. What We Do Not Use

Evera does not use advertising cookies, behavioral tracking, third-party analytics, or data-selling mechanisms. No personal profiling occurs on this site.

4. Consent

By continuing to browse Evera.world, you consent to the use of essential cookies strictly necessary for service functionality. Optional cookies are not deployed.

5. Managing Cookies

You can delete or block cookies in your browser at any time. The site will continue to function, but certain user preferences (language or accessibility) may be reset.

6. Legal Basis

Our cookie use aligns with the EU ePrivacy Directive (2002/58/EC) and GDPR Recital 30. As Evera operates as a global humanitarian educational project, these standards are applied universally.
