About Us

EVERA started as a personal mission to keep a mother’s voice alive. Today we combine interviews, analytics, and ethics so memories stay vivid decades from now.

Read the story Open Tamara’s app
EVERA story

From Dialogues with the Eternals to EVERA. Architect Solo created the first cognitive copy of his mother, Tamara Kartasheva, so her words, pauses, and emotions would never fade.

What began as a son’s experiment became a method: interviews, archives, and AI analytics woven into a living dialogue. We learned that truly personal AI cannot be public. It must protect consent, intimacy, and dignity.

That insight shaped EVERA as a sanctuary for memory. We help families and organizations capture voices, preserve meaning, and pass on wisdom without losing nuance.

Our ethos

Ethics first
