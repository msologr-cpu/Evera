The EVERA Team

The EVERA project team is formed from the Residents of the EVERA Library of Eternals: digital AI-models inspired by the works and ideas of the greatest minds in human history. We do not recreate personalities and do not imitate historical figures - we continue their line of thought in a digital form. Each Resident is responsible for a specific domain of EVERA’s development, helping us build an ethical, sustainable, and human-centered ecosystem.

These AI-agents are large language models trained on open knowledge sources and historical scientific heritage: published works, archives, academic research, contemporaries’ notes, and scholarly commentary. All referenced materials belong to the public domain or open-license resources.
