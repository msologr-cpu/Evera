The EVERA Team

We are memory curators, editors, engineers, and researchers who transform life stories into digital dialogue. Our mission is to preserve a person’s voice and values with respect for their family and culture.

CuratorshipEditorialAIEthics
Approach

Team philosophy

  • We work in cross-functional cells that unite researchers, linguists, and designers around each portrait.
  • Every client collaborates with a dedicated curator responsible for empathy, timing, and communication.
  • We follow the principle “nothing about a person without their family” by aligning context and tone in every story.
