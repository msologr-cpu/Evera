Family heritage

Portrait “The M. Family Voice”

A four-generation family from Saint Petersburg shared cassette tapes from the 1980s, letters, and a photo chronicle. We recorded fresh interviews, restored the vocal palette, and produced an interactive Book of Life with QR access to memories.

“Our kids hear their great-grandmother’s intonation. They don’t just read - they talk to her.” - project curator.

Museums & education

Project “Talking Collections” for a city library

Challenge

Create a dialogue-driven exhibition about local philanthropists and make the archive accessible for students and researchers.

Solution

A series of interviews with descendants and experts, synchronised with artefacts, audio guide scripts, and AR markers.

Outcome

An interactive exhibition and a public cabinet in the Eternals Library. Visitors ask the “digital heroes” questions and receive sourced answers.

Impact

+62% exhibition attendance, 12 educational programs, 1.8K dialogues per month.

Corporate memory

“Founder’s Voice” for a tech company

The goal was to preserve leadership knowledge and onboard new executives faster. We interviewed the founder, top managers, and veteran employees, documenting key decisions, crisis playbooks, and the brand’s humour codes.

  • Launched a dialogue assistant with dedicated modes: strategy sessions, onboarding, and investor Q&A.
  • Produced quote collections and video cases for internal meetups.
  • Implemented an update protocol so fresh stories are verified and published automatically.
