1. Purpose of the Project

Evera provides access to digital models of philosophical, scientific, and cultural figures (“Eternals”) for educational and research purposes. The dialogues available through Evera are generated using verified linguistic and historical sources. They are intended to encourage learning, reflection, and critical thinking — not to simulate or impersonate living persons.

2. Non-commercial Nature

Evera operates as a humanitarian and educational project. All content, dialogue modules, and digital personas are provided under a non-commercial, open-knowledge framework. Any commercial use, redistribution, or derivative works require prior written permission from the founder.

3. Intellectual Property

All source code, data structures, AI dialogue architectures (“Evera Format”), and visual materials are protected under international copyright and database rights. © Maximian Solomonidis (Architect Solo). Unauthorized reproduction or model training based on Evera data is strictly prohibited.

4. User Responsibilities

Users agree to engage respectfully in all dialogues and to use the platform for constructive, educational purposes. It is forbidden to attempt to modify, reverse engineer, or extract training data from the system. Evera does not collect personal data beyond what is necessary for functional analytics.

5. Data and Privacy

Use of this website constitutes acceptance of the Privacy Policy and Cookies Policy. Evera complies with GDPR and equivalent international frameworks. No sensitive personal data are retained or sold.

6. AI Transparency

All dialogues with the Eternals are algorithmically generated reconstructions. They do not express the opinions of any living person or institution. See AI Transparency & Identity Disclaimer for details.

7. Ethical Standards

Evera follows the Ethics Charter emphasizing respect for truth, science, and cultural diversity. Political propaganda, hate speech, or ideological manipulation are incompatible with Evera’s mission.

8. Accessibility

Evera strives for universal accessibility and inclusivity. For detailed information, refer to the Accessibility Statement.

9. Governing Principles

Evera is a global, non-jurisdictional project registered under digital authorship. Disputes are resolved by dialogue and consensus, in the spirit of philosophy. The applicable framework is the Open Knowledge License (Evera-OKL-1.0).

10. Contact

For inquiries, permissions, or ethical concerns, contact the founder directly: @solomonidis.
