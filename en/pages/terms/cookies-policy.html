Cookies Policy

At Evera we rely on the bare minimum of tracking technologies. This policy explains which cookies are used, why they exist, and how you can control them.

1. Types of cookies we use

  • Functional cookies — store language preferences and session state so the site stays in your chosen language and works reliably across pages.
  • Analytics cookies — limited traffic metrics collected in an anonymized form to understand server load and improve accessibility. There are no advertising or behavioral cookies.

2. Retention period

Functional cookies persist for up to 30 days to remember language and accessibility settings. Analytics cookies last no longer than 7 days and never contain personal data.

3. Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies via your browser settings. This may reset language preferences, but it will not restrict access to Evera materials.

4. Third-party services

We do not use third-party ad networks, social media pixels, or tracking beacons. Analytics is operated by Evera and follows the Privacy Policy.

5. Updates

If the cookies policy changes, the date and version will be updated here. Transparency is essential: no new category of cookies will be added without notice.

6. Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, contact: @solomonidis.
